Welcome to CavQuest! CavQuest is a UVA Scavenger Hunting app where you can find cool places and things on grounds, and learn more history and facts about them too!

Explore the Rich Heritage of UVA with CavQuest

With our app, embark on a journey to uncover the hidden stories behind each iconic building and learn intriguing facts that even some locals might not know. CavQuest transforms every exploration into an interactive and educational experience, connecting you deeply with the rich history and heritage of UVA. It's not just about the destination, but the fascinating stories and knowledge you'll gain along the way.
