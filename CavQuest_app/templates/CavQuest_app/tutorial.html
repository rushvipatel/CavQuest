TUTORIAL

HOW TO PLAY

To play, go to Home and click "View Hunts and Start Hunting." From there, select any hunt to view more information about it. Once you find one you like, click "Start Hunt!" You will need location permissions enabled before being able to start your scavenger hunt. Clicking "How far am I?" will tell you how close you are to the destination. If you are close enough, it will congratulate you on finishing the hunt! You'll be able to see a picture of the location and more fun facts about it. If you get stuck, click "Next Hint" for a little push in the right direction! Happy Hunting!

SUBMITTING A NEW HUNT DESTINATION

To submit a new hunt destination, go to Home and click "Add a New Hunt." From there, fill in the place or object's secret scavenger hunt name that you want other hunters to find, along with a description and three hints incase the hunter gets stuck. After you click submit, your submission will be reviewed by admin. If its approved, you will soon see your contribution added to the list of CavQuest hunts!
