About Us!

This project was created by UVA CS 3240 students Nicole Savage, Maria Rahmouni, Zhennguang Wang, Rushvi Patel, and Sunny Chanda.

Inspiration

Before beginning this project, we knew we wanted to create an app where users would need to find hidden locations. Working within the constraints of our project guidelines, we decided to incorporate Google Maps to build a scavenger hunt app that would take the user's current position and tell them how far away they were from the scavenger hunt destination. We based this scavenger hunt around buildings, statues, and popular places around UVA's Grounds, as we thought doing so would be a fun and educational experience for people to get to know Grounds just a little bit more.

Learning Along the Way

While working on this project, we've learned more about the importance of being adaptable when working on a team. We've also learned to not be scared of making mistakes, and instead embrace a learning opportunity that may begin in failure. A lot of work required for this project was uncharted terrority for our team, so we put a lot of effort into finding resources that would help us figure out the right ways to implement our desired features. We discovered a lot not only about software development, coding practices, and team building, but also about cool locations on Grounds! We hope you can learn something new, too!
